The Fool's Gold
 		 	 
The Fool's Gold was a legendary starship that pirate Bruce Mercy wanted to find. Mercy and his band of pirates attempted to steal the location of the Fool's Gold from Plako, but were beaten to the ship by a group of free-lancing adventurers.
