Pod 3 was a RK-392 mobile tac-pod that was utilized by the Consolidated Holdings of Preox-Morlana Corporation during the Imperial Era.&#91;5&#93; In 5 BBY,&#91;6&#93; the pod was destroyed on the planet Ferrix after Pre-Mor Authority Security Forces encountered the rebels Luthen Rael and Cassian Andor.&#91;5&#93;
