The Jin'ha starship was a spacecraft used by the Jin'ha on the worlds of Coruscant and Obredaan in 32 BBY for their operations in arms smuggling with the criminal organization Black Heth. Its color scheme was mostly red, and had two prongs attached to the main pod that contained the cockpit
