The Outrider was a YT-2400 light freighter owned by Dash Rendar&#91;4&#93; that left the planet Tatooine&#91;2&#93; in 1 BBY.&#91;5&#93; Like other YT-2400 light freighters, it was manufactured by Corellian Engineering Corporation;&#91;1&#93; could reach speeds of 800 kilometers per hour; was equipped with two double laser turrets, concussion missile launchers, and a class 2 hyperdrive;&#91;3&#93; and measured 18.65 meters.&#91;2&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			The Outrider leaving Mos Eisley in the A New Hope Special Edition
 		 	 
The Outrider was first introduced to the new Star Wars canon in the 2018 reference book YT-1300 Millennium Falcon Owners' Workshop Manual, written by Ryder Windham.&#91;2&#93; It was originally created for the 1996 Star Wars Legends multimedia series Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire where it was the personal ship of Dash Rendar.&#91;6&#93; It was later included in the 1997 Special Edition of A New Hope, where it could be seen leaving Mos Eisley shortly after Luke Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, C-3PO and R2-D2 arrive in the spaceport.&#91;7&#93;
