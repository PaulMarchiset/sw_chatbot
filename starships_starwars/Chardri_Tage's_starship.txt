A ship was owned by Chardri Tage and his partner and co-pilot, Tamsin, in the year after the formation of the Galactic Empire. Bail Organa sent the pair in their starship to locate a former Jedi, Ahsoka Tano, though he did not tell them whom he sought. They located her on a planet in the Thabeska system when her freighter was pursued by an agent of the Black Sun criminal syndicate. They fired upon the other ship, forcing the agent to flee, then locked Tano's in a tractor beam.&#91;1&#93;
