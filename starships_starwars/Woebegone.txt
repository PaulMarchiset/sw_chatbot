Woebegone was a starship captained by Ellin Lah often traded goods on Carlix's Folly, Bal'demnic, Sikkem IV, and Vestral. It was stolen by Darth Plagueis in 67 BBY to escape Bal'demnic after an illegal cortosis mining operation resulted in the death of Darth Tenebrous. The crew of the ship was killed when Plagueis attacked them for trying to check his identification with the authorities. He stole the ship's medical droid before having the ship sealed and slagged when he docked at Deep Space Demolition and Removal.&#91;1&#93;
