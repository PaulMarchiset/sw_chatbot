The Espirion Multi was a red-colored starship used by natives of the planet Espirion during the Galactic Civil War between the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire. When a fleet of Alderaanian ships gathered in Espirion's orbit came under attack from an Imperial-class Star Destroyer, the Alderaanian-Espirion hybrid Beon Beonel had the ship aid the Alderaanians after hearing a speech by Princess Leia Organa broadcast from her shuttle Lord Junn. The ship's captain contacted the Alderaanians upon entering the combat and told them to stay back while they handled the Imperials. They then attacked the Star Destroyer and destroyed it.&#91;2&#93;

The crew of Espirion Multi included individuals of multiple species, like Ithorian and Gand.&#91;2&#93;
