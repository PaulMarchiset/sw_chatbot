A gigantic, planet-sized probe was created millions of years ago by an ancient race from an unknown galaxy with the mission to examine all evolved life forms in the universe and to suppress those which endangered the balance established by the Force. 

This section of the article assumes completion of a roleplaying game adventure seed or suggestion.

The probe eventually made its way to the galaxy of the Galactic Empire where it was discovered by a group of pathfinders. The group ventured inside the probe where they faced all manor of traps and danger. They eventually made it to the center of the probe where they met an old man named God.&#91;1&#93;
