The Kintan Comet was a starship that Fensi Moisk served as a crew member aboard in 22 BBY.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Kintan Comet was first mentioned in the 2013 visual guide, Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Ultimate Illustrated Guide, which was written by Jason Fry. The book was originally written in English and slated to be published, but only found a Danish release as Star Wars: Klonernes Angreb Den Ultimative Illustrerede Guide. Jason Fry confirmed on Bluesky that the Danish caption "Kintansk Comet-mandskabsuniform" beside Fensi Moisk's picture was originally written in English as "Kintan Comet crewer's uniform" where Kintan Comet would have been the name of a starship.&#91;2&#93;
