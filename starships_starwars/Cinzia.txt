The Cinzia was a starship built on Sebaddon around the time of the Cold War. It had been sent by Lema Xandret to establish a peace treaty. Some years after the Treaty of Coruscant, the Cinzia was confronted by the smuggler Jet Nebula in the Auriga Fire, but the starship, controlled by Fastbreeders, self-destructed. Nebula was able to recover the ship's navigation computer, as well as a compact fastbreeder factory that produced the deadly droids.
