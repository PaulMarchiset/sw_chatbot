The red apricot was the starship of red apricot squad, an elite squad of the Knights of Silver Dawn, a prominent civil mercenary group in the Aakaash system. Attended to by the squad's mechanic, Zhuang Yuanying, as well as a team of seven worker drones of the WB-35 mechanic droid model, the red apricot was shaped like a cone lying down on its side. Its elongated contour was protected by an armored hull composed of enigmatic iron and other metals, with the hull being disposable in a maneuver known to red apricot squad as "shell shedding."&#91;1&#93;

When the squad was tasked to retrieve a lost Silver Dawn shipment of enigmatic tenebrae, the squad took the red apricot to Fire and later Lake 4399 in the Heaven system.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The red apricot appeared in The Vow of Silver Dawn, a Chinese web novel written by "His Majesty the King." The ship first appeared in a chapter released in January 4, 2021, and is named 红杏号 in the original simplified Chinese text.
