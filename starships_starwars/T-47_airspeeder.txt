Characteristics[]

"No, no, not the T-47! I can fly anything, but that doesn't mean I want to. Piloting the T-47 is like steering a block of wood."
&#8213;Poe Dameron&#91;5&#93;

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Luke salvaging equipment from his crashed snowspeeder on Hoth.
 		 	 
The T-47 airspeeder, also known as the T-47 light airspeeder,&#91;11&#93; was a model of low-altitude vehicle manufactured by Incom Corporation.&#91;1&#93; The vessel was designed for civilian usage and saw use hauling sewage,&#91;8&#93; but they would go onto see service as military craft. All the same, the vessel was not a particularly maneuverable craft, to the extent that Resistance pilot Poe Dameron&#8212;despite saying that he could, if needed, fly the ship&#8212;compared piloting a T-47 to trying to steer "a block of wood."&#91;5&#93;

A small, wedge-shaped vehicle, the T-47 was a two-person craft crewed by a pilot and a rear-facing tailgunner,&#91;7&#93; with a top speed of 1,100 kph.&#91;6&#93; At least in the case of its modified, military form, it had two forward-facing heavy laser cannons and a harpoon cannon placed in the rear and manned by the tailgunner.&#91;7&#93;

History[]

"Yeah, well, we're not soldiers. Those speeders are for hauling sewage."
&#8213;Sully Stark&#91;8&#93;

In 9 BBY,&#91;12&#93; T-47s were used to haul sewage but saw use by the Hidden Path. When Obi-Wan Kenobi and Tala Durith rescued Leia Organa, Sully Stark, and Wade Resselian helped them escape from Fortress Inquisitorius by strafing the stronghold's landing platform in their T-47s. However, Reva Sevander threw an explosive using the Force at Resselian's airspeeder which caused it to explode, killing him.&#91;8&#93;

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			A trio of T-47s cripple an Imperial AT-AT on Sullust.
 		 	 
During the Galactic Civil War, the Partisans had access to T-47s during their insurgency on Jedha.&#91;9&#93; During the Battle of Hoth, Rebel Alliance snowspeeders used tow cables to disable All Terrain Armored Transport (AT-AT) walkers by firing the tow cable and chasing it out around the walker's legs, stopping its locomotion and immobilizing the walker.&#91;2&#93; At some point, three T-47s attacked and crippled an AT-AT on Sullust.&#91;13&#93;
