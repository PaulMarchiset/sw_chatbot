The Morgana was a starship that went missing after entering hyperspace fifty cycles&#91;1&#93; before 3 ABY.&#91;2&#93; A human smuggler claim to have seen the Morgana in the Sayma Nebula while talking with an associate at a cantina in the nearby space station Hauler's Keep.&#91;1&#93;

This section of the article assumes 100% game completion&#32;of Star Wars Outlaws. Any alternate stories may be noted in the "Behind the scenes" section. The events in this section may or may not have been confirmed as canon.

	

A thief named Kay Vess overheard this conversation and later recovered old galactic credits from a container near where the smuggler claimed to have seen the Morgana.&#91;1&#93;

Assumption ends here.

Behind the scenes[]

The Morgana was first mentioned in the "A Pirate's Fortune" expansion for the Massive Entertainment video game Star Wars Outlaws,&#91;1&#93; which was released on May 15, 2025.&#91;3&#93; It was mentioned as part of an ambient conversation that players can listen to to start the "Ghost Ship" intel quest.&#91;1&#93;
