The Rueful Confession was a starship that had its test flight&#91;1&#93; in 1 BBY.&#91;2&#93; Tabor Seitaron, an Imperial captain who taught military history to cadets at Carida Academy, was among those on board during the flight. Four years later, when he took a shuttle to&#91;1&#93; the Imperial-class Star Destroyer&#91;3&#93; Herald, Seitaron looked back at the test flight when trying to recollect the last time he had experienced artificial gravity.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Rueful Confession was mentioned in the 2015 novel Battlefront: Twilight Company, written by Alexander Freed.&#91;1&#93;
