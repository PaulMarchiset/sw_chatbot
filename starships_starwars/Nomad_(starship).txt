The Nomad
 		 	 
A Nomad was a starship that arrived at systems just after a battle that damaged repair facilities to compensate for this and aid citizens of the affected systems. It had a repair bay large enough to handle multiple vessels.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Nomad in the graphic novel adaptation appears to have been modeled after a Star Galleon-class frigate, a ship first appearing in the Legends novel The Last Command.
