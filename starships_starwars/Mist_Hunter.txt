The Mist Hunter&#91;4&#93; was a modified Byblos Drive Yards&#91;1&#93; G-1A starfighter&#91;2&#93; owned by the bounty hunter Zuckuss,&#91;1&#93; used by him and his companion 4-LOM. Holding cells aboard Mist Hunter were used to hold Beris Ford and Han Solo after his capture on the planet Valtos.&#91;4&#93; It later crash-landed during a pursuit of Solo and the Wookiee Chewbacca.&#91;5&#93;
