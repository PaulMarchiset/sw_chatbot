The G30 racing starfighter was a type of racing ship manufactured by Incom-Freitek. The starfighter's S-foils could be adjusted for better aim of the wingtip-mounted Taim & Bak KX10B laser cannons or to accommodate the need for speed or maneuverability.&#91;1&#93;  The Green Ace flown by racer Hype Fazon was a modified G30.&#91;4&#93;
