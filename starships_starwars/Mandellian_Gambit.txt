The Mandellian Gambit was a starship active during the Great Galactic War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire. Risha Drayen, the daughter of the crime lord Nok Drayen, was born aboard the Gambit&#91;1&#93; in 3664 BBY.&#91;2&#93; The ship's name recalled a famous incident early in Nok Drayen's career, where he massacred ten thousand pirates at the planet Mandellia.&#91;3&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Mandellian Gambit was mentioned in the 2012 reference guide Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia.&#91;1&#93;

↑ Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia gives Risha Drayen's age during Star Wars: The Old Republic as 21 years old, though it does not specify at what point in the game's events she is that age. However,  Star Wars: The Old Republic &#8212; Conversation with Early Independence: "Risha Drayen" establishes that she was 11 years old when Nok Drayen went into carbonite, and  Star Wars: The Old Republic &#8212; Smuggler Mission: "The Endless Abyss" on the Long Shadow, which is the finale of Act I for the Smuggler class, states that Drayen was in carbonite for 10 years. As Act I occurs in 3643 BBY, simple math confirms that Risha Drayen was 21 during the events of Act I, and puts her birth in 3664 BBY.
