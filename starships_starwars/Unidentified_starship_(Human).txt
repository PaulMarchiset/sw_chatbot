A starship with a crew of at least one was owned by a Human male scoundrel. The scoundrel lost the starship to an Ithorian and a Baragwin in a game of holochess.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The starship was first mentioned in the attribution of an image drawn by Chris Trevas in the 2009 book The Essential Atlas, written by Jason Fry and Daniel Wallace.&#91;1&#93; The scoundrel was an in-universe self-portrait of Trevas. The book's editor, Erich Schoeneweiss, came up with the idea of having the Human lose his starship as a joke on Trevas.&#91;2&#93; The image was used to illustrate the varying spacefaring species.&#91;1&#93; The 2009 Scavenger's Guide to Droids sourcebook pictured the Human scoundrel flying a starship, but it is not stated that this is the starship described in this article.&#91;3&#93;
