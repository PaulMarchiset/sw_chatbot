The Bitemark was a starship that was purchased by the Trandoshan bounty hunter Bossk&#91;1&#93; by 3 ABY.&#91;2&#93; Shortly after the purchase, the ship was destroyed during an explosive firefight on the Jurzan spaceport between the bounty hunter and the smugglers Han Solo and Chewbacca.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Bitemark was mentioned in "Tooth and Claw," a short story written by Michael Kogge and published in the From a Certain Point of View: The Empire Strikes Back anthology in 2020.&#91;1&#93;
