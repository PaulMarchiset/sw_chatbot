A starship was used by the Duros trader Staaysha Dekkr who worked for the Duro Interstellar Freight and Shipping company. The starship became trapped in a gas cloud nebula after attempting to make a shortcut through it to get to Klytus V Station. Dekkr and the starship were later rescued by Resistance pilot Tallissan Lintra.&#91;1&#93;
