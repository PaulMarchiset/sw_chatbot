The Possibility was a name given to a Nomad Four, a civilian version of a Seltaya military courier ship, owned by the former handmaiden Duja. It crashed on Mokivj, having been shot down by a Separatist vulture droid during the Clone Wars. The ship, being piloted by Padmé Amidala, was previously damaged in a pursuit with Batuu police. Possibility was the name broadcast by the vessel's ID beacon during the time that Amidala piloted it, though she was unaware of its real name.&#91;1&#93;
