The Silver Dawn was a starship used by the Silver Knights, a group of mercenaries led by Li Yu alongside protocol droid White. The ship was instrumental during Jedi Master Mostima and her Padawan Sean's investigation of the political and familial intrigue in the Aakaash system&#91;1&#93; around 200 BBY.&#91;2&#93;
