The Queen of Ranroon was a long-lost starship. The Crimson Dawn crime lord Dryden Vos had an ancient navi-computer dataplaque onboard his yacht, First Light, with glyphs that allegedly pinpointed the location of the vessel.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The legend of the Queen of Ranroon first appeared in the Legends continuity novels Han Solo's Revenge & Han Solo and the Lost Legacy by Brian Daley. In the novels, the vessel was a treasure ship of the powerful pre-Republic warlord, Xim the Despot. It was said to have vanished while loaded with a vast fortune, inspiring searches by treasure hunters for millennia .
