This was a light, black transatmospheric ship that came to the aid of Pharika on Sakuub and was intent on taking the Sakuub Holocron after being led to it by Jaina Solo and Jagged Fel. It killed Pharika after she was defeated but was then destroyed by an Unidentified Hutt in his G1-M4-C Dunelizard fighter. Its occupants were unknown.

Specifications[]

The ship was described by Jagged Fel as compact and sleek, and completely unknown to anything he had ever seen before. The ship could also move with "astonishing speed" and was equipped with a Laser weapon. However, it was no match for the power of the Dunelizard's Laser cannon.
