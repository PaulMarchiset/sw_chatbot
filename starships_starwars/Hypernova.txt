The Hypernova&#91;3&#93; was the starship of Balada the Hutt. It floated over the Grand Arena on Vespaara.&#91;1&#93;

 This article is a stub about a ship or starship. You can help Wookieepedia by expanding it.
	

Behind the scenes[]

Hypernova first appeared in the video game Star Wars: Hunters,&#91;3&#93; which soft-launched on November 16, 2021 to select countries.&#91;4&#93; However, the ship was unnamed until update 0.18.0, through the Arena Pass as one of the rewards available.&#91;3&#93;
