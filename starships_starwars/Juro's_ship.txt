Juro's ship&#91;2&#93; was a starship owned by Jedi Master Juro. After a skirmish on the Hy Izlan Aerial Temple, the starship arrived near the temple and was boarded by Juro, alongside Force-sensitives Ethan, Lah Kara, and the redeemed Homen.&#91;1&#93;
