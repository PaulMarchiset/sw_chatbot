The Ravenstar was a starship flown by the explorer Ariana Surabat and her crew, who founded the planet Batuu in the Outer Rim Territories.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Ravenstar was created for Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, which opened to guests at Disneyland on May 31, 2019.&#91;1&#93; The ship first appeared in the story "The Skiff & the Galleons," which was published in the Galaxy's Edge edition of Myths & Fables written by George Mann. The art of the story, that included a picture of the Ravenstar, was illustrated by Grant Griffin.&#91;2&#93;
