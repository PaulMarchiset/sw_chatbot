Description[]

Xosad Hozem's starship was used by Hozem and his crew who were a part of Saw Gerrera's Partisans. The ship had a cockpit with room for at least two people, a back section with jump seats for passengers, and a common area with benches and seats surrounding a table that was screwed into the floor. Past the common room was a hallway that led to a room with bunks for Hozem's crew, namely Jari and Bilder. The floors of the ship were rough and dull metal, as was most of the rest of the interior.&#91;1&#93;

It was compatible to dock with a CCR-class pinnace via a transport tube.&#91;1&#93;

History[]

Hozem piloted the ship for a mission to gather information from a civilian transport that the Empire was using as a scout ship. Joined by Jari, Bilder, Gerrera, and Jyn Erso, they traveled from Wrea to the Western Reaches and boarded the ship, stealing datapads of intel before returning to Gerrera's outpost.&#91;1&#93;
