A starship was captained by Haplif, an Agbui agent of the expansionist Grysk species in the Unknown Regions. Aboard the ship, Haplif led a group of 50 members of his Agbui species conducting a plot to destroy the Chiss Ascendancy on behalf of the Grysk agent "Jixtus." During their travels, the Agbui came to host two Chiss aboard the ship: the betrothed mid-agers Yomie and Coduyo'po'nekri.&#91;1&#93;
