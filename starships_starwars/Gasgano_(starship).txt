"Our ship bears a resemblance to the shyyyo bird, but we didn't want to call it that, so we named it after our favorite podracer, Gasgano."
&#8213;B8-T5, in journal of Ardis San Tekka&#91;1&#93;

Gasgano was a starship that belonged to Galactic Society of Creature Enthusiasts Director of Creature Cartography Ardis San Tekka and her safari droid companion B8-T5. The vessel resembled a shyyyo bird, but the pair did not wish to use that as a name and instead named the vessel after their favorite podracer,&#91;1&#93; the Xexto&#91;2&#93; Gasgano. When observing animals, B8-T5 recorded data and audio for the pair to analyze back on the Gasgano. B8-T5 then mentioned the Gasgano in an entry on whisper birds in San Tekka's journal&#91;1&#93; at some point after the Battle of Exegol in 35 ABY.&#91;3&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Gasgano was mentioned in the 2024 reference book Star Wars Bestiary, Vol. 1: Creatures of the Galaxy, which was written by S.T. Bende.&#91;1&#93;
