The Corsair was a starship from which Jaxxon T. Tumperakki stole hypercoils. The Lepi smuggler put the coils in his own ship, the Rabbit's Foot, but told the Pirate Queen Maz Kanata that the hypercoils had been stolen by Lando Calrissian.&#91;1&#93;
