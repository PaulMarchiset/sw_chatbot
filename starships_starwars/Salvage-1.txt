Salvage-1 was a starship owned by the droid Captain Spikewheel, who led a crew aboard the ship. When a group wanted to join the crew of the ship, Spikewheel took them to Lotho Minor, forcing them to stay one night on the junk planet in order to prove themselves.&#91;1&#93;
