The Vakkian Wing was a rumored starship with a cursed crew.&#91;1&#93; In 3 ABY,&#91;2&#93; a humanoid member of the Rokana Raiders invoked the Vakkian Wing as a reason to be concerned of the sounds they heard while guarding the Khepi Tomb.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Vakkian Wing was mentioned in the "A Pirate's Fortune" expansion for Massive Entertainment video game Star Wars Outlaws,&#91;1&#93; which released on May 15, 2025.&#91;3&#93;
