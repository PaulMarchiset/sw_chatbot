The Rampart was a ship that had been stolen by Tobias Beckett. He used it after stealing identichips on Hovun IV, but was forced to destroy it on the space station Munt Ontdal to prevent Enfys Nest from getting her hands on them.&#91;4&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Rampart first appeared in the one-shot comic Beckett 1, which was published by Marvel Comics&#91;4&#93; on August 15, 2018.&#91;5&#93; The issue was written by Gerry Duggan with Marc Laming and Will Sliney illustrating the starship. The Rampart shared its name with the&#91;4&#93; 2011 film Rampart, which starred Woody Harrelson as detective Dave Brown.&#91;6&#93; Harrelson portrayed Tobias Beckett in the Anthology film Solo: A Star Wars Story,&#91;7&#93; which released May 25, 2018.&#91;8&#93;
