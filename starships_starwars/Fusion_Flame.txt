The Fusion Flame was a starship&#91;1&#93; from the waning decades of the Galactic Republic.&#91;2&#93; It once raced another ship, later called the Stellar Envoy, around the planet of Drall and was soundly defeated. The victorious pilot recorded the victory in the winning ship's SuperFlow IV droid brain. In 18 BBY, the ship that had beaten the Fusion Flame, by then given the name Stellar Envoy, was being restored by a mechanic named Bammy Decree. He discovered the pilot's record of the race against the Fusion Flame while analyzing the brain.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The Fusion Flame was mentioned in Millennium Falcon, a 2008 novel by James Luceno.&#91;1&#93;
