The Rodrigo Andrera was a minesweeper craft tasked with dismantling the Palpatine Mine Network sometime after the Battle of Endor. After an accident, only two crew members were found alive aboard the vessel, one of whom was Ebenn Q3 Baobab.&#91;1&#93;
