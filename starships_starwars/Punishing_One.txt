"There she is--isn't the Punishing One beautiful? If the Millennium Falcon could do the Kessel Run in under twelve parsecs, then she could probably do it in ten."
&#8213;Dengar&#91;3&#93;

The Punishing One was a JumpMaster 5000 owned by the bounty hunter Dengar.&#91;2&#93; The ship had no holding cell due to the fact that Dengar usually only took in dead bounties.&#91;4&#93;

At one point, Dengar took the Punishing One to the planet Canto Bight, where he gambled, including playing sabacc against several Weequay pirates. Soon after,&#91;3&#93; in 3 ABY,&#91;5&#93; Dengar and fellow bounty hunter Beilert Valance, who were begrudgingly working together, returned to Canto Bight following a mission on the moon of Nar Shaddaa, reaching the Punishing One. However, they had been tracked by the Crimson Dawn assassin Deathstick.&#91;3&#93;
