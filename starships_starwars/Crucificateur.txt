Behind the scenes[]

The French language utilizes a grammatical gender system and thus both Les Barons d'Acier&#160;: trois capitaines pirates and La guerre des loups articles use the masculine Crucificateur as opposed to the feminine Crucificatrice in naming this vessel.&#91;2&#93;&#91;1&#93;
