A ship was used by Jedi Hunters as they pursued a Jedi ship. The Jedi ship escaped, but the Jedi Lah Kara was blasted into space and eventually drifted to an abandoned ship. The Jedi Hunter ship arrived at the abandoned ship, where Kara was again able to escape the Jedi Hunters. The ship's hull was red, and it was equipped with blaster cannons and deflector shields.&#91;1&#93;
