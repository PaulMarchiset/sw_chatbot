Tac-Pod 2 was a RK-392 mobile tac-pod that was utilized by the Consolidated Holdings of Preox-Morlana Corporation during the Imperial Era.&#91;5&#93; In 5 BBY,&#91;6&#93; the pod was used by Pre-Mor Authority Security Forces to travel to the planet Ferrix in order to arrest the criminal Cassian Andor.&#91;5&#93;
