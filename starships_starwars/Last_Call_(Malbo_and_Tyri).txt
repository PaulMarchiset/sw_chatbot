The Last Call was a starship that was used by the Sector Rangers Malbo and Tyri. When the Rangers sent a group of agents to recover some data from a Joruba Consortium compound on the planet Nyriaan, they agreed to pick the agents up in the Last Call, once the mission had been completed.
