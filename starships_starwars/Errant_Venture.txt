The Errant Venture was a starship with a dedicated casino.&#91;1&#93; By 21 ABY,&#91;2&#93; the gambler Lando Calrissian had made one of his best hauls playing in the casino, which he considered to have a good gambling hall.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

In the current Star Wars canon, the Errant Venture was mentioned in the 2022 novel Shadow of the Sith, which was written by Adam Christopher.&#91;1&#93; In the Star Wars Legends continuity, the Errant Venture was a Star Destroyer originally named the Virulence before its capture by the smuggler Booster Terrik, first appearing in the 1997 novel X-Wing: The Bacta War by Michael A. Stackpole, the fourth book in the Star Wars: X-Wing series.&#91;3&#93;
