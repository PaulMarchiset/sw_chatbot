The Degarian II was one of Senator Crote's fastest starships; used in the years leading up to the Battle of Naboo. From Frego, Crote allowed Jedi Knights Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi to take the ship to Coruscant while escorting Lena Cobral there so she could testify against the Cobral family. Wary of an attack, the Jedi opted to find a different means of passage back to the capital. When the Jedi arrived on Coruscant, they learned that the Degarian II had been attacked and destroyed while en route; there were no survivors.
