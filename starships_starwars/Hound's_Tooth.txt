The Hound's Tooth was a modified Corellian Engineering Corporation YV-666 light freighter used as the personal starship of Bossk, a Trandoshan bounty hunter.

Description[]

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Bossk escapes an explosion aboard the Hound's Tooth in his Z-95 Headhunter, Nashtah Pup.
 		 	 
The Hound's Tooth was a modified YV-666 light freighter manufactured by the Corellian Engineering Corporation.&#91;1&#93; The elongated, rectangular hull was topped with the ship's command bridge, giving the pilot a commanding view through the segmented viewport.&#91;6&#93; A deflector shield generator was situated below the bridge, and a forward ramp allowed access into the interior, as did a side ramp close to the aft maneuvering fins. A rather grim painted mascot adorned the side of the forward hull, depicting a female Trandoshan standing betwixt the ship's name in Aurebesh, with a battle-axe strapped to her back, a blaster in one hand and a Wookiee's severed head in the other.&#91;5&#93;&#91;7&#93;

Within, the vessel's lower decks were taken up by the engines, power core, and weapon systems.&#91;6&#93; It was equipped with a quad laser cannon turret, an ion cannon, and a concussion missile launcher. The Hound's Tooth could reach speeds of up to 1,190 kph, and had a class 1.5 hyperdrive. Bossk used his ordinary-looking freighter to store his huge collection of weapons, interrogation tools, and gruesome trophies from his years as a bounty hunter. He transformed the YV-666's cargo bays into high-security prison cells, and installed an advanced security system that ensured anyone attempting to break in would be blasted.&#91;5&#93; One of the cargo holds, residing on the ship's upper level,&#91;2&#93; was transformed into a launch bay for his personal Z-95-AF4 Headhunter, Nashtah Pup.&#91;8&#93;

History[]

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			The Hound's Tooth docks in the Quarzite space station.
 		 	 
Bossk used the Hound's Tooth as his personal starship,&#91;4&#93; modifying the ordinary-looking vessel for his far-from-ordinary work as a bounty hunter.&#91;5&#93;

In 20 BBY, members of Krayt's Claw, including Boba Fett, Dengar, C-21 Highsinger, Latts Razzi, and Asajj Ventress used Bossk's ship to travel to the Quarzite space station orbiting the planet Quarzite to accept a job.&#91;9&#93;

During the Age of the Empire, Bossk flew the Hound's Tooth to the Outer Rim planet of Lothal to collect a bounty on the Dug criminal Gronson Takkaro. For much of that trip, the ship remained at the spaceport of Lothal's Capital City.&#91;1&#93; At another time, the ship suffered an explosion that forced Bossk to abandon the freighter in his personal Z-95-AF4 Headhunter, the Nashtah Pup.&#91;8&#93;
