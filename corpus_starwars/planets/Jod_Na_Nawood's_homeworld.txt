A planet was the homeworld of Jod Na Nawood, a human male pirate by the New Republic Era. The planet was known for being covered in fog, or "ashy dust" like Na Nawood described. Upon landing on At Achrann with the Onyx Cinder crew and their droid, Na Nawood commented that At Achrann's fog felt similar to his homeworld's.&#91;1&#93;
