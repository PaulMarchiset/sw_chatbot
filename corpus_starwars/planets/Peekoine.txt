Peekoine was a planet. There were four Landslashers located on the planet. Landslashers 1, 2, and 3 maintained the canals of the planet. Landslasher-4 carved new canals. It was orbited by the space diner Mother's. Peekoine was home to to The Gutterwipes, a swoop gang.
