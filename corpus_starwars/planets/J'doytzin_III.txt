J'doytzin III was a planet in the galaxy. During the Clone Wars, Chancellor Palpatine negotiated with their Shahmistra. The meeting was so tedious in Palpatine's eyes that he promised himself J'doytzin III would be among the first worlds to feel the wrath of the Galactic Empire when it rose.&#91;1&#93;
