In 0 ABY,&#91;2&#93; Storm-hair&#91;3&#93; Warlord Nuso Esva traveled aboard his Kazellis-class light freighter Salaban's Hope to an unknown and unremarkable terrestrial planet that orbited an unremarkable sun for more than five hours before returning to the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Chimaera. Senior Captain Thrawn of the Galactic Empire speculated that the world was where Esva met with his commanders to finish their preparations for a battle against the Empire in the Candoras sector's Poln system, a theory the senior captain shared with the former smuggler Jorj Car'das after the battle.&#91;1&#93;
