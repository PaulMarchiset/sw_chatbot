A planet was used&#91;1&#93; between 3 ABY and 4 ABY&#91;2&#93; by Crimson Dawn members "The Archivist" and Kho Phon Farrus as the second site where they attempted to unlock the Fermata Cage.&#91;3&#93; It housed at least one jungle and ocean.&#91;1&#93; After their attempt failed, the Sith Lord Darth Vader arrived at the planet to retrieve the Cage and kill the two, though he was interrupted by the arrival of the Orphans led by Chanath Cha, who had been sent by Crimson Dawn leader Lady Qi'ra&#91;3&#93; to rescue them and the Fermata Cage. Cha and Sear battled Vader, whilst Ladybright and Imara Vex were ordered by Cha to get the Archivist, Farrus, and the Cage to safety. Cha and Sear were both killed by Vader, and Ladybright was destroyed by the Archivist after she refused to follow Qi'ra's orders of leaving the planet. The Archivist then took control of the Orphans ship, the Siroto, and departed from the planet with Farrus and the Feramata Cage onboard.&#91;1&#93;
