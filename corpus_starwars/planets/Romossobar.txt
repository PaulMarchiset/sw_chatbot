	Place of Enlightenment Through Knowledge&#91;1&#93;
Romossobar was a planet in the galaxy that was the site of the Place of Enlightenment Through Knowledge. During the Cold War, the Jedi Master Tarben Jenoquan was studying on Romossobar when he was briefly infected with the Dark Plague.&#91;1&#93;
