Utapau was a planet in the Kessil system.

History[]
The Fourth Moon of Utapau was the refuge of Kane Starkiller and his sons Annikin and Deak after the Jedi Rebellion. When a Sith Knight discovered their hiding place, and Deak was killed, Kane and Annikin were forced to flee to Aquilae.
