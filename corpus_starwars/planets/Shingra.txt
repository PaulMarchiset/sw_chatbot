Shingra
	Astrographical information
		System
	Ordali system&#91;1&#93;
Shingra was the only inhabitable planet in the Ordali system.&#91;1&#93; It is covered in snowy mountains, with an average temperature of -15°C. Rarely, gems renowned for their size and purity can be found by mining the rock here. The primary spaceport is a small town of Kommba, mainly constructed of local conifer wood. Atmospheric flying is discouraged due to constant turbulence, and repulsorlifts are unreliable due to magnetic materials in the ground.
