Around 41.5 ABY,&#91;2&#93; a Damorian S18 light freighter was forced to land on planet after being ambushed by the Sith Meditation Sphere Ship and five Sith from the Lost Tribe of Sith. After staying behind at the ship's landing site, Sith apprentice Vestara Khai discovered a Human girl who had gone unnoticed. Khai killed her, making the girl, who Khai dubbed "First," the first person Khai had ever killed. The planet was temperate, with open grassy plains that had patches of mud and soil speckled throughout and forested hills.&#91;1&#93;
