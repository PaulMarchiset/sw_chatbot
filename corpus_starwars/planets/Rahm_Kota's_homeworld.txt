The homeworld of Rahm Kota, this planet was a war-torn place. The population of the planet were engaged in trench warfare for years, and Kota saw combat from the age of ten. Eventually, the Jedi Mace Windu arrived on the planet to negotiate a peaceful end to the fighting. During his time of the planet, he discovered Kota's Force sensitivity, and took him to Coruscant to be trained as a Jedi. Kota was eighteen at the time.&#91;1&#93;
