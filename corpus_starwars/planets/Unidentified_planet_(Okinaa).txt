A planet was the location of a village that the Sith Okinaa lived in. Okinaa lived on the planet for at least ten years, and was one of the last remaining members of the village along with their droid, R5-D56. Okinaa was killed by the Ronin in a duel that began in a holy place on the planet.&#91;1&#93;
