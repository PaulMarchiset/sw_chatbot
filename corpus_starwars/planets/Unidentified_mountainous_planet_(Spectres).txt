A mountainous planet was visited by the Spectres rebel cell during the Imperial Era. Rebels Sabine Wren, Hera Syndulla, and Chopper attempted to steal a medical kit from a Lambda-class shuttle on the planet; however, Galactic Empire stormtroopers captured the shuttle while pursuing the Sprectes. Wren and Syndulla then stole another shuttle while Chopper captured the stormtroopers' before fleeing.&#91;1&#93;
