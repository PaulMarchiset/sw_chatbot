History[]
Before the High Republic Era,&#91;2&#93; the Evereni put their trust in their stormy homeworld of Everon in an attempt to live in harmony with nature; however, the Great Storm came, in the form of a vortex storm that rose from the planet's core bringing strong winds and lightning, destroying the native Evereni's civilization.&#91;3&#93;
A series of centuries-long conflicts in the aftermath were fought among the Evereni for the remaining resources following the Great Storm. This brought the Evereni to near-extinction, forcing those who survived to flee the planet around 500 BBY&#91;2&#93; and become the Wanderers. With his people long ago having fled their homeworld,&#91;3&#93; the later Evereni Marchion Ro felt that the closest thing had to a home was his flagship, the Gaze Electric.&#91;4&#93;
