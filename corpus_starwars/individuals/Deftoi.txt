The Deftoi were individuals that inhabited the world Muntro VI, in the Outer Rim Territories.&#91;1&#93;
Every ten years, the Deftoi of Muntro VI organized their famous Otherworld Arts Festival.&#91;1&#93; For the 1 ABY&#91;2&#93; edition of the festival, the Deftoi requested the Office of the Music Advisor of Naboo to provide carcasses of Naboo animals in order to hold a performance depicting the events of the Time of Suffering of Naboo.&#91;1&#93;
