The Hellhulk was the nickname of a cybernetic Chthonic worm god that guarded a hellhulk station in the workshop of the Skakoan Techno Union chairman Wat Tambor on the planet Skako Minor. The god was kept dormant until a techno-totem was disturbed by an Imperial Delta-class T-3c shuttle. It was crushed under a roof by Dek-Nil.&#91;2&#93;
