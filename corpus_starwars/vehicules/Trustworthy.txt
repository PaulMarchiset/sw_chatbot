The Trustworthy was a starship flown by the pilot Reen and her droid partner Kreven. The two were hired by the Guild of Technicians to transport cargo aboard the vessel, but Kreven sabotaged the thrusters, intending to allow pirates to steal the Trustworthy's cargo. After the Trustworthy crashed, an Ewok stowaway caused havoc throughout the ship, and discovered a comlink that revealed Kreven's betrayal to Reen.&#91;1&#93;
