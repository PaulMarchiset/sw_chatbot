In 0 ABY,&#91;2&#93; Doss's ship, which was equipped with an engine, traveled where the Hand of Judgment stormtrooper deserter group was held prisoner by its owner, the mercenary Mikhtor Doss. Shortly after, Emperor's Hand Mara Jade killed Doss's forces and inquired the mercenary's client, the politician Bok Yost, where Hand of Judgment member Korlo Brightwater was being held. Yost then stated that the former stormtrooper was aboard his TL-1800 freighter Melnor Spear, not aboard Doss's ship.&#91;1&#93;
