This gold spacecraft was a vessel used by C-3PO and R2-D2 on a trip to Tatooine after being stranded on another planet. The wedge-shaped ship featured an socket for an astromech droid and a single pilot's seat. The craft's engine required space crystals to function. When stranded, the droids needed to retrieve new space crystals from a cave when the ship's supply had been depleted.&#91;1&#93;
