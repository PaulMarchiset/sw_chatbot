Tarasin Dream was a merchant ship that was registered on Cularin and operated during the Clone Wars.
History[]
On one occasion during the Clone Wars, the Dream was traveling as part of a convoy when it dropped out of hyperspace and entered the Cularin system. However, it was attacked by some Thaereian military Z-95 Headhunters, which claimed that it was running contraband. The Dream could not fight back, as it was unarmed, so it sent out a distress. This was detected by the Heroes of Cularin, who attacked the Thaereian Headhunters and tried to save it.
