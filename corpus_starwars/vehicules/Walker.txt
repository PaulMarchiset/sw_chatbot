All Terrain Attack Pod[]
 Main article: All Terrain Attack Pod
The All Terrain Attack Pod (AT-AP) was a three-legged walker initially used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars.&#91;13&#93; They were later used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War against the Galactic Empire.&#91;7&#93;
All Terrain Tactical Enforcer[]
 Main article: All Terrain Tactical Enforcer
The All Terrain Tactical Enforcer (AT-TE) was a hexapodal walker used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. The walker had six laser cannon turrets, a heavy projectile mass-driver cannon, and legs that could be magnetized to climb up and down walls.&#91;14&#93;&#91;15&#93;
All Terrain Reconnaissance Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Reconnaissance Transport
The All Terrain Reconnaissance Transport (AT-RT) was a small bipedal walker used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. The walker had an open cockpit, and a single chin-mounted laser cannon. Two of these could be stored in the back of an AT-TE. They were also used by the Empire, the Rebel Alliance, and the First Order.
All Terrain Open Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Open Transport
The All Terrain Open Transport (AT-OT) was an octapodal walker used by the Galactic Republic toward the end of the Clone Wars.&#91;13&#93; Its open top allowed it to transport more troopers across the battlefield.&#91;15&#93;

Imperial walkers[]
All Terrain Armored Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Armored Transport
The All Terrain Armored Transport (AT-AT) was a quadrupedal walker used by the Galactic Empire after the fall of the Galactic Republic.&#91;23&#93; With a height of over 20 meters,&#91;24&#93; the walker relied heavily on intimidation.&#91;25&#93;
All Terrain Armored Cargo Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Armored Cargo Transport
The All Terrain Armored Cargo Transport (AT-ACT)&#91;26&#93; was a quadrupedal walker used by the Galactic Empire that featured a dedicated cargo bed for the transportation of heavy building materials or combat munitions.&#91;27&#93; These vehicles shared a similar design with the AT-AT, although they were larger to accommodate storage space and featured less weaponry.
All Terrain Armored Cannon[]
 Main article: All Terrain Armored Cannon
The All Terrain Armored Cannon, or AT-AC, was a bipedal walker that served in the Galactic Empire. The walker was the forerunner to the AT-ST and AT-DP.&#91;28&#93;
All Terrain Anti Aircraft[]
 Main article: All Terrain Anti Aircraft
The All Terrain Anti Aircraft was a heavily armored quadrupedal walker utilized by the Galactic Empire for anti-air defense.&#91;29&#93;
All Terrain Defense Pod[]
 Main article: All Terrain Defense Pod
The All Terrain Defense Pod (AT-DP) was a bipedal walker used by the Galactic Empire primarily for patrols and scouting missions.&#91;30&#93; The walker could seat two crew members; a driver and a gunner.&#91;31&#93; A variant of the AT-DP was the AT-DP Arc Cannon Prototype, which carried the Arc Pulse Generator, a superweapon.&#91;32&#93;
All Terrain Defense Turret[]
 Main article: All Terrain Defense Turret
The All Terrain Defense Turret (AT-DT) was a bipedal walker used by the Galactic Empire in instances such as the Mimban Campaign.
All Terrain Force Reconnaissance Walker[]
 Main article: All Terrain Force Reconnaissance Walker
The All Terrain Force Reconnaissance Walker was a bipedal scouting walker utilized by the Galactic Republic's Advanced Recon Commando units as well as the Imperial Army.&#91;33&#93;
All Terrain Missile Platform[]
 Main article: All Terrain Missile Platform
The All Terrain Missile Platform (AT-MP) was a bipedal walker used by the Galactic Empire 3 years after the Battle of Yavin. The AT-MP was heavily armed with missiles and had an even heavier variant; the AT-MP Mark III.&#91;7&#93;
All Terrain Scout Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Scout Transport
The All Terrain Scout Transport (AT-ST) was a bipedal walker used by the Empire as means to eliminate smaller forces.&#91;34&#93; They were often deployed along with the larger AT-ATs to back them up and eliminate smaller forces.&#91;35&#93; The AT-ST had a more heavily-armored variant known as the AT-ST Mark III.&#91;7&#93;
All Terrain Personal Transport[]
 Main article: All Terrain Personal Transport
The All Terrain Personal Transport (AT-PT) was a small bipedal walker used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War, against the Rebel Alliance.&#91;15&#93;
Scuttler[]
 Main article: Scuttler
The Scuttler was a prototype walker used by the Visler Korda's faction late in the Galactic Civil War.&#91;9&#93;
OI-CT[]
 Main article: OI-CT
The OI-CT was a model of crane walker used by the Empire notably on Corellia and in the construction of Fortress Vader on Mustafar.
