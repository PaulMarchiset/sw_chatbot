This Galleon was, according to legend, a starship that participated in a competition to map a safe route to the planet Batuu and back during the early days of space travel. However while on route the galleon was attacked by pirates and left a drift. Its captain, a Rodian named Baloch, was taken by the pirates to be ransomed. While its one remaining crew member, a human named Kreth, was found and rescued by the crew of the Ravenstar.&#91;1&#93;
