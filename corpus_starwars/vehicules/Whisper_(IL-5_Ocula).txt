The Whisper was an IL-5 Ocula starfighter built by the Czerka Corporation. It was later purchased by the Hutt Cartel, who modified it with a variable transponder code system, basic stealth features, and a secure communications array.&#91;1&#93;
Mandalore the Avenger seized the ship after her forces discovered it involved in a smuggling operation in Mandalorian space. Her technicians further improved the ship's stealth systems and scanners.&#91;1&#93; The ship was used during the Mandalorian civil war to travel to Ruhnuk&#91;2&#93; and also used to rescue Darth Malgus from Carrick Station.&#91;3&#93;
