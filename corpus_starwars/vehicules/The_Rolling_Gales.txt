The Rolling Gales&#91;2&#93; was a starship that served as the mobile headquarters and concert stage of the band Star Waver. It was piloted by the droid K-344 and carried her fellow band members Lan Ar-Shoo, Jay, V-5, and Geezer Odajion.&#91;1&#93;
The Rolling Gales sustained damage when bounty hunter Boba Fett pursued the band in Slave I, after which Fett took Geezer as a bounty for Jabba Desilijic Tiure. All the other band members cooperated to repair their ship.&#91;1&#93;
