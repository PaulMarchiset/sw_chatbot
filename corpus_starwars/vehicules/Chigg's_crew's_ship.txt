A ship was operated by the crew of the Abednedo Chigg during the High Republic Era. At one point, the crew traveled to the planet Galjaero to acquire an exotic fruit but their ship got stuck in mud. With help from Jedi younglings from the planet Tenoo, the crew were able to recover their ship and traveled with the Jedi to the space station Starlight Beacon.&#91;1&#93;
