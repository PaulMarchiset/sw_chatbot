The Brightfox was a starship that participated in the Karee Blockade Clutch&#91;1&#93; in 7 ABY,&#91;2&#93; where it was beat by the Vorantis, a ship owned by the pilot Brantis Mo Fresk. Later that year, Mo Fresk offered his services as a pilot for hire on Frander's Bay&#8212;a space station that orbited the Core Worlds planet Chandrila. When the New Republic operative Han Solo visited Frander's Bay to hire an unaffiliated pilot with his friend Lando Calrissian, Mo Fresk's barker mentioned the pilot's beating of the Brightfox in the Karee Blockade Clutch into one of Solo's ears.&#91;1&#93;
