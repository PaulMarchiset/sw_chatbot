The Twilight Explorer was a starship affiliated with the Jedi Order that was lost at the bottom of Lake Paonga, on the Mid Rim planet Naboo. Between around 232 and 230 BBY&#91;2&#93;, the droid 0G-LC and his associates discovered the long-lost wreckage of the ship, and sought the valuable Jedi star compass within for historical research. 0G-LC tasked Jedi Initiates Nubs, Kai Brightstar, and Lys Solay, along with the Gungan explorer Kluurt to recover the artifact. News of the ship's location made its way to the Gungan thief Tooba Jinx, who hoped to obtain the star compass and sell it. The Jedi and Jinx raced to the wreckage of the ship, and found the star compass on the ship's bridge.&#91;1&#93;
