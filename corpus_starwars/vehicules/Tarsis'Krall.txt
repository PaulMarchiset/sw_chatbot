The Tarsis'Krall
The Tarsis'Krall was a starship that crashed in the Dune Sea on Tatooine. During the Cold War, a group of prisoners escaped from the Imperial Reclamation Service with the help of Jedi, and fled into the desert. Officer Vengo activated the prisoners' shock collars, forcing them to take shelter in the ruins of the Tarsis'Krall and tend to the injured.
