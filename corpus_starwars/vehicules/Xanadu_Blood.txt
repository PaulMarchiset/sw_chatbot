The Xanadu Blood was a modified Rogue-class Porax-38 starfighter owned by the Duros bounty hunter Cad Bane. The Xanadu Blood was given to Cad Bane by Darth Sidious as payment for his theft of a holocron and the kyber memory crystal as well as his usual rate times three.&#91;3&#93; Bane used it during his kidnapping of Wee Dunn from Rodia and Zinn Toa from Glee Anselm, and lost the ship when he was captured.&#91;5&#93; Bane regained custody of his ship and used it to hunt down Ziro the Hutt.&#91;6&#93; At some point, Bane replaced the Xanadu Blood with a new ship, the Justifier.&#91;7&#93;
The Xanadu Blood was primarily green in color with yellow and red markings.&#91;3&#93;
