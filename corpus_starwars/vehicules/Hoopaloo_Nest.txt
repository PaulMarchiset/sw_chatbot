The Hoopaloo Nest was a starship owned by the informant Graysom. While waiting to meet Kanan Jarrus and Hera Syndulla in an asteroid field, the Nest was taken over by an entity, causing it to crash on an asteroid. The abandoned ship was later found by Jarrus and Syndulla, who landed on the asteroid to explore it, unaware that the entity had transferred itself into their ship, the Ghost.&#91;1&#93;
