A starship was used by Bennet Sten and Myler Sten, who brought food and medical aid to those in need across the Outer Rim Territories. They both died and left the starship to their son Yuko. Yuko's caretaker droid BILY intended to give him the ship when he grew up, but showed it to him and his friend Sola sooner after pirates attempted to find a treasure left behind by Yuko's parents.&#91;1&#93;
