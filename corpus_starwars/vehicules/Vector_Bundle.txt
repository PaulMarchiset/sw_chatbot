The Vector Bundle was a starship owned by the Starros Clan that was kept at their ancestral home on Hosnian Prime.&#91;2&#93; Between 3 ABY and 4 ABY,&#91;3&#93; when the house was attacked by Imperial captain Cerasus Ehllo in order to retrieve his wife Aryssha, Thea Starros, Mevera Starros, and Sana Starros used the Vector Bundle to escape from the building.&#91;2&#93;
The three Starroses flew the Vector Bundle to the Hosnian sector where they were ambushed by Marl Jibs. Mevera used an ion cannon to disable the bounty hunter's ship. The ion cannon was a weapon that Mevera had confiscated from a group of Soikans after they failed to pay her from some freelance work. The Starroses then flew the Vector Bundle to the Dalnan sector.&#91;1&#93;
