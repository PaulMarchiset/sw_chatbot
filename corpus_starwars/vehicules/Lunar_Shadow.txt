	Usage
		Commander(s)
	Lunar Shadow Captain
Lunar Shadow was a ship stuck on Nar Shaddaa during the Meetra Surik's visit to the planet. It was crewed by Nikto, and the captain was a Weequay who frequented the Jekk'Jekk Tarr. The Nikto were unable to leave because the captain was also their pilot, and they were unable to go fetch him due to the fact that the cantina contained fumes that were poisonous to their species.
