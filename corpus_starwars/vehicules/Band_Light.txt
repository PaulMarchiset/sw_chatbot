The Band Light was a vessel suspected of privateer activities during the reign of the Galactic Empire. The vessel was commanded by a captain during the Imperial period, and the crew consisted of Nix&#8212;who was second in command&#8212;Pend, and Red. The crew attempted to salvage a derelict Etti-class light transport in the vicinity of the planet Ord Mantell&#91;1&#93; by 1 BBY,&#91;3&#93; and they began to die in what appeared to be a series of accidents which were caused by at least one Imperial Terror Trooper. In the wake of the deaths of Nix, Pend, and Red, the Band Light's captain decided to abandon the Etti-class vessel. The captain soon noticed additional problems with the Band Light&#8212;such as lights switching off and doors opening on their own&#8212;and then sent a transmission about the incident, which was intercepted.&#91;1&#93;
