Description[]
The Finalizer was a Resurgent-class Star Destroyer and was 2,915.81 meters, the standard length for an Resurgent-class Star Destroyer. As such it was armed with over 1,500 turbolasers and ion cannons, point-defense turrets, point-defense missile emplacements and tractor beam projectors&#91;1&#93;
History[]
Early years[]
The Finalizer was secretly built by the First Order in their "distant orbital factories"&#91;17&#93; in the years after the Battle of Endor,&#91;5&#93; which was in violation of the Galactic Concordance.&#91;12&#93;
It was the site of the advanced training for older stormtrooper cadets. After the Parnassos warrior Phasma was brought into the First Order inner circle by General Brendol Hux, Captain Cardinal was marginalized and the training was turned over to Phasma.&#91;8&#93;
Cold War[]
Attack on Jakku[]
The ship was under command of General Armitage Hux during Kylo Ren's assault on the planet Jakku in an attempt to obtain Luke Skywalker's hiding position from a Lor San Tekka, an ally of the Resistance. When the Resistance fighter, Poe Dameron, was captured during the assault, the Finalizer remained in orbit during his interrogation.&#91;5&#93;
 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Hux on the Finalizer
During Dameron's escape, he and the deserting stormtrooper, FN-2187, stole a TIE/sf space superiority fighter from one of the vessel's hangars and managed to disable the Finalizer's turbolasers. During their escape, the Battlecruiser was only able to seriously damage the TIE, without fully destroying it. It was later present when Starkiller Base fired on the Hosnian system, with Ren watching the blast from the ship's bridge.&#91;5&#93;
First Order-Resistance War[]
Attack on the Resistance base[]
The Finalizer later participated in the hunt for the Resistance fleet after D'Qar was evacuated shortly after Starkiller Base's destruction. It was under the command of Captain Edrison Peavey during this time due to General Hux being transferred to Supreme Leader Snoke's flagship, the Mega-class Star Dreadnought Supremacy.&#91;9&#93;&#91;13&#93;
Pursuing the Resistance fleet[]
It later participated in the First Order Navy's attack on the fleeing Resistance fleet.&#91;13&#93;&#91;9&#93; With the damage sustained to the Supremacy in the battle,&#91;13&#93; the Finalizer took over as the First Order flagship.&#91;18&#93;
Kylo Ren’s ceremony[]
 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			Snoke’s remains float into space from the hangar
After the escape of the remaining Resistance forces from the Battle of Crait, Kylo Ren held a ceremony aboard the ship officially becoming the new Supreme Leader of the First Order, using the force to lift the remains of the bisected previous leader Snoke above the heads of attending stormtroopers and blaming the Resistance for his death, finally pushing his body out of the hangar to float into space.&#91;18&#93;
Destruction of Tah'Nuhna[]
The Finalizer, along with 17 other Resurgent-class Star Destroyers and a Mandator IV-class Siege Dreadnought, participated in the destruction of the planet Tah'Nuhna as they had contact with Resistance members.&#91;6&#93;
Final action at Batuu[]
Eventually, the Finalizer was present at Batuu, although after an attack left it crippled, it was retired as the main flagship of the First Order in favor of the Steadfast.&#91;10&#93;
Commanders and crew[]
General Hux and Captain Peavey presided over bridge operations on the Finalizer. However, the ship was, in a formal sense, the personal flagship of Kylo Ren,&#91;15&#93; who also exerted control over operations.&#91;5&#93;
