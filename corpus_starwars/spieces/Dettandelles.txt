"For that matter, few are ferocious or relentless as me. Lesser captains would shrink like wilting dettandelles when confronted with the fearsome Two-Headed Toothvole of Bogmadada. Not I. I steeled my courage and shot the creature right between its eyes. Then right between the other eyes!"
&#8213;Hondo Ohnaka in the first draft of his holobook "Hondo's Memoirs"&#91;1&#93;

Dettandelles were a species that could wilt. In the first draft of his holobook, "Hondo's Memoirs", the pirate captain Hondo Ohnaka used the phrase "shrink like wilting dettandelles" to describe an individual being cowardly.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

Dettandelles were first mentioned in a datapad entry that players could find in the "Lost Treasures" quest of the "A Pirate's Fortune" expansion for the Massive Entertainment video game Star Wars Outlaws,&#91;1&#93; which was released on May 15, 2025.&#91;2&#93;
