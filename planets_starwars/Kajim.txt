Kajim

[Source]

Kajim was a planet in the galaxy. During the Cold War between the Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic, an automated Imperial surveillance ship crashed on Kajim, though Imperial operatives were able to drive off the Republic retrieval team and reclaim it for the Empire.&#91;1&#93;
