Soum was a planet located in the galaxy. It was the homeworld of the feline sentients known as the Souma.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

Soum was only mentioned in Les démons de feu, a roleplaying adventure published in the 25th issue of French gaming magazine Graal.&#91;1&#93;
