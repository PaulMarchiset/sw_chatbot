Arges was a planet that was believed to be the homeworld of the arges frog, a type of frog that had come to inhabit the forests and deserts of several other worlds, including Nevarro, Ossus, and Arvala-7, by the New Republic Era. Galactic Society of Creature Enthusiasts Director of Creature Cartography Ardis San Tekka mentioned Arges when writing about arges frogs in her journal&#91;1&#93; at some point after the Battle of Exegol in 35 ABY.&#91;2&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

The planet Arges was mentioned in the reference book Star Wars Bestiary, Vol. 1: Creatures of the Galaxy, which was written by S.T. Bende&#91;1&#93; and released on November 5, 2024.&#91;3&#93; Prior to the book's release, the page mentioning Arges was first revealed in a StarWars.com article published on October 22, 2024.&#91;4&#93;
