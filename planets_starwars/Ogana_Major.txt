Ogana Major, or Ogana, was a huge blue Planet in the Kessil system. It was orbited by five moons, including the Fourth Moon of Ogana.&#91;1&#93;

It was the homeworld of The Starkiller, and was destroyed by the Galactic Empire before Luke Starkiller could reach it.&#91;2&#93;

It was a desert world, above which Princess Leia was captured by the Empire.&#91;3&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

"Ogana" replaced Utapau in the first draft of the script for A New Hope.&#91;1&#93; In the second draft, Ogana Major was the name for what would become the planet Alderaan.&#91;2&#93; It became known as "Organa" in the synopsis of the third draft and again stood in for the future Tatooine.&#91;3&#93; In the third draft, Organa Major had the role that Ogana Major had in the second draft and was Leia's homeworld. Additionally, Organa was Leia's surname in the third draft&#91;4&#93; and all subsequent drafts. In the fourth draft and all subsequent drafts, the planet called Organa Major in the third draft was called Alderaan.&#91;5&#93;
