Vald was a planet which Grand Moff Lotter was once responsible for enslaving. However, after the defeat of the Galactic Empire by the New Republic, he ordered the genocide of the local populace. When the New Republic heard word of this, they sent a group of agents to track him down.&#91;1&#93;
