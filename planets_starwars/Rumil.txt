Rumil was a planet in the galaxy. During the Cold War between the reconstituted Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic, personal blasters were banned on Rumil. The Exchange sensed an opportunity, and wished to hire someone to deliver black market weapons to Rumil.&#91;1&#93;
