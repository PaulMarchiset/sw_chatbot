A planet was the homeworld of the human smuggler Chita.&#91;1&#93; At some point between 19 BBY and 0 ABY,&#91;2&#93; the Galactic Empire arrived to the world and, according to Chita, "made a mess" of the planet and her family, viewing the event as awful. Later,&#91;1&#93; at some point between 2 BBY and 0 ABY,&#91;3&#93; Chita told the former Jedi Gleenu of her homeworld when talking of the planet Zenarita's struggle under Imperial rule, mentioning they were not the only ones that held a grudge against the Empire.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

Chita's homeworld was mentioned in "The Smuggler," a non-canon short film released as part of the third volume of the Star Wars: Visions series. The animated short, produced by Trigger, was released on Disney+&#91;1&#93; on October 29, 2025.&#91;4&#93;
