Seluket was a planet that featured numerous stone monuments that were centuries old by the time of the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the reconstituted Sith Empire. During the Cold War, glowing patterns appeared on the monuments, and investigations into the monuments revealed that the patterns warned of an impending ecological disaster.&#91;1&#93;
