Ristal was a planet in the Kubilaï system. The colonel Ershack commanded a Rebel base on the planet sometime after the Battle of Yavin. After the disappearance of an Ytterbium cargo which was destined to the Rebel garrison on Hoth, he sent Rebel operatives to investigate.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

Ristal was created by Jean Balczesak for the ambiguously canon roleplaying scenario L'écume de l'espace which appeared in the French magazine Casus Belli 44.&#91;1&#93;
