Malorin was a planet inhabited by a group of the same name. During the High Republic Era, the world had a trading agreement with the Oisinians of the planet Oisin. Malorin eventually became trapped within the Occlusion Zone, the territory of the Nihil marauders surrounded by the Stormwall barrier, cutting off the Malorins' trade with the Oisinians.&#91;1&#93;
