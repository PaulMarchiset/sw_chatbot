Promencious Four was a planet. Around the time of the rule of the Eternal Empire, the centuries old wreck of the Splendor, a lost Mirialan luxury cruiser, was discovered in the lava fields of Promencious Four.&#91;1&#93;

Behind the scenes[]

Promencious Four was mentioned in Star Wars Legends in Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Eternal Throne, the sixth digital expansion of the 2011 BioWare game Star Wars: The Old Republic.&#91;1&#93; Promencius Four originated in the 2015 canon novel Battlefront: Twilight Company, written by Alexander Freed.&#91;2&#93;
