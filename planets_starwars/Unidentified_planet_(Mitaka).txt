A lifeless planet was the site of the Jedi Mitaka's compound, where the human and several of his assistant droids resided. Within the structure, Mitaka and his assistants researched how to bring life to the planet. One day, one of the droids, T0-B1, found a skyhopper in the basement of Mitaka's compound and asked through comm if there were any Jedi present. His message reached an Inquisitor of the Galactic Empire, who landed on the lifeless world and&#8212;as T0-B1 suspected&#8212;killed Mitaka.&#91;1&#93;

Shortly after, T0-B1 left Mitaka's residence and found the late Jedi's grave, putting fragments of the late Jedi's lightsaber within it. The droid resumed researching how to create life and eventually succeeded, bringing grass and rain to his homeworld. While admiring the rain with the other droids, the Inquisitor who had killed Mitaka arrived in a TIE/ad v1 to engage T0-B1, who he believed was a Jedi. The droid then reconstructed Mitaka's lightsaber and engaged the Imperial agent in a lightsaber duel, killing the Inquisitor. Soon after, T0-B1 and another droid, C03, left the planet aboard Mitaka's skyhopper, looking to populate other lifeless planets.&#91;1&#93;
