Duzay was a world in the galaxy.&#91;1&#93; In 1 ABY,&#91;2&#93; a group of tourists from Duzay sojourned at the Lake Retreat Hotel during their stay on Naboo. An employee of the hotel named Rovim Minnoni recruited a spacer to rescue these tourists when they got lost during a walk in the wilderness.&#91;1&#93;
