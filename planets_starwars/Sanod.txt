Sanod was a planet beyond the borders of the Galactic Empire that was a haven for trafficking of the narcotic Blue. During the Galactic Civil War, a group of Rebel investigators went under cover on Sanod to find the source of the drug trafficking. As part of their cover, they were given an internship in administrative techniques at the spaceport in Istix, the planet's capital and only city.

Behind the scenes[]

Sanod was created, along with Elbriade and the planet KX-112 as part of the French role-playing game supplement in the magazine Casus Belli.
