This article's subject originated in a source that was released outside of the Lucas Licensing process, and thus is considered non-canon by Lucasfilm.

	

	

	

KX-112 was a small planet that orbited a star away from any of the major commercial routes. It was associated with the Galactic Empire and the trafficking of the narcotic Blue. During the Galactic Civil War, a group of Rebel investigators went under cover to the planet Sanod to find the source of the drug trafficking. Their investigation led them to this small planet.

Behind the scenes[]

KX-112 was created, along with Sanod and the planet Elbriade as part of the French role-playing game supplement in the magazine Casus Belli.
