Talman III was a hot desert planet known for its mining activities and relative lawlessness. Some time during the Galactic Civil War, the planet contained a warehouse that supplied the Imperial logistics station in the Rama system with air filters as well as a spaceport and a nightclub.&#91;1&#93;
