A planet was the original homeworld of the Lepi Lop. After Lop escaped being enslaved by the Galactic Empire, she was adopted by the Yasaburō clan and came to consider the planet Tao to be her homeworld. Lop later mentioned the planet to her sister Ochō.&#91;1&#93;
