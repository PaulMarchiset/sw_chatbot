Rokirex was a planet in the galaxy and the homeworld of the Krex species. Despite being widely considered the Krex's native world, their ancient mythology referred to their ancestors as travelers, and it was theorized that the Krex were descendants of some Chistori colonists who settled on Rokirex.&#91;1&#93;
